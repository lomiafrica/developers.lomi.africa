# Activate License

Below are the steps to activate an Personal license.

### Step 1: Click on the key icon in lomi.

The key icon is located at the bottom left corner of the lomi. window. Click on the key icon to open the license management window.

![login](/screenshots/license-management/golden-edition/individual/click-license-icon.png)

### Step 2: Select Personal License

Select the personal option to continue with the personal license activation.

![login-otp](/screenshots/license-management/golden-edition/individual/select-personal-license.png)

### Step 3: Enter License details

Enter the license key and your email and click on the `Activate` button to activate the license.

![login-otp](/screenshots/license-management/golden-edition/individual/enter-license-details.png)

### Step 4: Enter OTP

Enter the OTP sent to your email.

![login-otp](/screenshots/license-management/golden-edition/individual/enter-activate-otp.png)

### Step 4: License Activated

Once the license is activated, you will see a success message.

![login-otp](/screenshots/license-management/golden-edition/individual/license-activated.png)

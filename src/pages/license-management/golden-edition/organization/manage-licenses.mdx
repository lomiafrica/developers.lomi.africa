# Manage Organization Licenses

You can manage your organization's licenses by visiting [lomi. License Manager](https://license.usebruno.com/).

Below are the steps to manage your organization's licenses:

### Step 1: Login to lomi. License Manager

Please enter your email to login to lomi. License Manager.

![login](/screenshots/license-management/golden-edition/organization/login.png)

### Step 2: Enter the OTP

Please enter the OTP sent to your email.

![login-otp](/screenshots/license-management/golden-edition/organization/login-otp.png)

### Step 3: Manage Licenses

You can view and manage your organization's licenses here.

![login-otp](/screenshots/license-management/golden-edition/organization/user-licenses.png)

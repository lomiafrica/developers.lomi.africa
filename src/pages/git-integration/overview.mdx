# Contributing to lomi.

Welcome to the Contributing Guide for lomi., an open-source payment orchestration platform designed to simplify payment processing across Africa.

We believe in the power of open-source collaboration and welcome contributions from developers, designers, and payment enthusiasts who share our vision of transforming the payment landscape in Africa.

By contributing to lomi., you can:
- Help improve the platform's features and functionality
- Develop new payment integrations
- Solve technical issues and bugs
- Enhance the platform's security
- Expand lomi.'s reach and impact

In the following sections, you'll find information on how to get started with contributing, our contributing guidelines, the bounty program, and our code of conduct.

We appreciate your interest in contributing to lomi. and look forward to collaborating with you!

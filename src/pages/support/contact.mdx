# We're here for you !

lomi. is committed to providing exceptional support to our community and customers. We offer multiple channels for getting help, asking questions, and connecting with our team.

## Getting help

### GitHub support

For technical questions, bug reports, and feature requests, our GitHub repository is the best place to start:

- **Questions**: Open a [new question](https://github.com/lomiafrica/developers.lomi.africa/issues/new?labels=question) to get help from our team and community
- **Bug Reports**: Found an issue? [Report it](https://github.com/lomiafrica/developers.lomi.africa/issues/new?labels=bug) and we'll investigate
- **Feature Requests**: Have an idea? [Share it](https://github.com/lomiafrica/lomi./issues/new?labels=enhancement) with us

### Community support

Join our vibrant community of developers and users:

- **Discord Community**: Connect with fellow developers and our team in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/33syDfh9)
- **Documentation**: Browse our comprehensive [documentation](https://developers.lomi.africa) for guides and references
- **Contributing**: Help improve lomi. by submitting pull requests or reporting issues on [GitHub](https://github.com/lomiafrica/developers.lomi.africa)

### Direct support

# What is lomi.?

lomi. is a unified, open-source payment orchestration platform built to transform payment processing across West Africa. We seamlessly integrate multiple payment providers and methods while providing a comprehensive set of products for merchants.

Our platform empowers businesses to focus on growth without worrying about the challenges of payment processing.

We aim to solve two core challenges:

### **Payment Complexity**

The West African payment landscape is fragmented, with numerous payment providers, methods, and regulatory requirements. This complexity often forces merchants to manage multiple integrations, leading to increased development costs, maintenance overhead, and technical debt.

lomi. solves this by providing:

- **Enterprise grade Analytics**: Gain valuable insights through detailed transaction reporting
- **Simple Reconciliation**: Simple reconciliation and refunds processes
- **Powerful Dashboard**: Powerful dashboard for business management

### **Security and Compliance**

Merchants need robust security measures and compliance frameworks, but implementing these without compromising user experience or increasing operational complexity is challenging, especially in the West African context.

lomi. addresses this through:

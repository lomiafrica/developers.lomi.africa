# **Community**

## **Feedbacks**

At lomi., we believe that the best products are built together with users' and contributors' input. In our case, our growth and evolution are essentially driven by real merchant performance and needs.

**Help us build West Africa's most comprehensive and merchant-friendly payment orchestration platform!**

### **Share your experience**
- Report bugs and suggest features on [GitHub](https://github.com/lomiafrica/lomi./issues)
- Support us and share your [integration process](https://www.producthunt.com/products/lomi/reviews/new)
- Help improve our [documentation](https://github.com/lomiafrica/developers.lomi.africa)
- Star our project on [GitHub](https://github.com/lomiafrica/lomi./) to help us reach more developers and chat with us on [socials](https://x.com/lomiafrica)

### **Build with us**
- Contribute to our [open source platform](https://github.com/lomiafrica/lomi./)
- Create and share plugins/extensions/features/designs/logic, anything
- Help other developers in the community
- Submit pull requests

### **Stay connected**
- Read our [Blog](https://lomi.africa/blog) for updates and to learn more about us
- Attend our community events (stay tuned!)
- Follow us on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/lomiafri/)
- Build with our [IDE: Jumbo](https://jumbo.lomi.africa)

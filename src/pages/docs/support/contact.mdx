# **We're here for you !**

lomi. is committed to providing exceptional support to our community and customers. We offer multiple channels for getting help, asking questions, and connecting with our team.

## **Getting help**

### GitHub support

For technical questions, bug reports, and feature requests, our GitHub repository is the best place to start:

- **Questions**: Open a [new issue](https://github.com/lomiafrica/lomi./issues/new?labels=question) to get help from our team and community
- **Bug reports**: Found a bug or facing a problem? [Report it](https://github.com/lomiafrica/lomi./issues/new?labels=bug) and we'll investigate.
- **Feature requests**: Have an idea? [Share it](https://github.com/lomiafrica/lomi./issues/new?labels=enhancement) with us.

### Community support

Join our vibrant community of developers and merchants:

- **Documentation**: Browse our [documentation](https://developers.lomi.africa) for guides and references.
- **Discord**: Connect with fellow developers and our team in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/33syDfh9).
- **Contributing**: Help improve lomi. by submitting pull requests or reporting issues on [GitHub](https://github.com/lomiafrica/website).

### Direct support

Need to reach us directly? We're here to help:

- **Email support**: Contact us at [hello@lomi.africa](mailto:hello@lomi.africa)
- **Business inquiries**: For partnership or business-related questions, email the same address [hello@lomi.africa](mailto:hello@lomi.africa) and we'll get back to you promptly.
- **Schedule a call**: Book a [30-minute call](https://cal.com/babacar-diop-umkvq2/30min) with us.
